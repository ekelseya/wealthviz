How rich are they?

The wealth gap in the US is hard to imagine. This is my attempt to visualize it.

Let's look at the majority of American incomes

Every square is 10 x 10 pixels and represents approximately $10,000

$12,760 - The poverty line for a single person household.

$30,680 - The poverty line for a family of five.

$63,179 - The median household income.

$90,021 - The mean household income.

$184,292 - The 90th percentile.

$248,728 - The 95th percentile.

$300,800 - The 99th percentile.
